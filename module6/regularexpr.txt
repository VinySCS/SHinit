   For learn more:
   
    ^ - The "^" means that it is the beginning of a line 
    $ - The "$" means that it is the end of a line
    [] - This is used in case of necessity to create a list of values
   ^[] - Gives all the lines that beginning with the values inside the list
   [^] - When the circumflex is inside a list in the beginning it means "reject all values" after the circumflex
     . - Joker that represents a character
     + - The before digit needs to appear again
     | - Or operator
    .* - Joker for anything
     * - The before digit can appear in any quantity
   {,} - The before digit needs to appear in the indicated quantity
